Ragdoll Cat Breed(布偶貓)

Characteristics:

They have a large head in a softer wedge shape than the sharp angular face of the Siamese.With a long, soft coat, oval-shaped blue eyes and medium-sized ears with rounded tips, their appearance matches their docile personality. They have a solid neck with a large and long, yet solid body, supported by a sturdy bone structure.

Lifespan:

12 to 17

Shedding:

They shed moderately throughout the year and more heavily in spring and fall.
